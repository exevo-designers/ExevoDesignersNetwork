Community Guidelines

Exevo Designers Network — Standards of Excellence

At Exevo Designers Network, we are building a professional, ethical, and growth-driven creative ecosystem. These guidelines define how designers are expected to operate within our platform and represent the Exevo brand.

Professionalism First

All designers must communicate and operate with respect, integrity, and accountability. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or unprofessional conduct is not tolerated. Your behavior reflects not only you, but the Exevo Designers Network as a whole.

Creative Integrity

  • All work must be original or legally licensed.
  • Plagiarism, copying, or misuse of creative assets is strictly prohibited.
  • Proper credit must be given to collaborators and sources where applicable.

Quality Standards

Designers are expected to deliver work that meets professional design standards and follows the project brief precisely. Revisions should be completed promptly and professionally.

Payment & Revenue Model

Designers earn 80% of the total project fee. Exevo retains 20% as agency share for acquisition and growth.

Payments released after client payment, work approval, and quality verification.

Client Ownership & Protection

All clients provided by Exevo belong to the agency. Designers must not contact clients privately or negotiate independent side deals.

Bypassing Exevo results in immediate removal.

Accountability & Confidentiality

Designers must respect deadlines. All client information, project materials, and Exevo internal processes are strictly confidential.

Community Culture

Exevo is a creative hub. Designers are encouraged to support one another, share knowledge, and build a growth-focused environment.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Immediate suspension and loss of earnings for: Client Poaching, Plagiarism, Fraud/Dishonesty, or Breach of Confidentiality.
